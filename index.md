Hi there! Thanks for visiting my page! 

I am Pramit, a [MITACS Globalink Graduate Fellow](https://www.grad.ubc.ca/campus-community/meet-our-students/saha-pramit), currently studying [Master’s of Applied Science (MASc)](https://www.ece.ubc.ca/policies-and-procedures/masc/masc) in the [Electrical and Computer Engineering Department (ECE)](https://www.ece.ubc.ca/) at the [University of British Columbia (UBC)](https://www.ubc.ca/), [Vancouver](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vancouver), Canada. I am a graduate research assistant in the [Human Communication Technologies Laboratory (HCT Lab)](https://hct.ece.ubc.ca/), supervised by [Prof. Sidney Fels](https://www.ece.ubc.ca/faculty/sid-fels). I have recently been awarded the prestigious [Young Scientist Award](http://www.miccai.org/about-miccai/awards/young-scientist-award) by MICCAI Society. 

I am also working with [Prof. Bryan Gick](https://linguistics.ubc.ca/person/bryan-gick/), the director of [UBC Interdisciplinary Speech Research Lab (ISRL)](https://blogs.ubc.ca/isrl/) and the head of [Department of Linguistics](https://linguistics.ubc.ca/), UBC as well as [Prof. Muhammad Abdul-Mageed](https://mageed.arts.ubc.ca/), the director of [UBC Natural Language Processing (NLP) Lab](https://github.com/UBC-NLP). Besides being a graduate research assistant, I am also acting as the [Treasurer and Executive Committee Member (2018-2020)](http://gsa.ece.ubc.ca/contact/current-executives/) of the [ECE Graduate Student Association (ECEGSA)](http://gsa.ece.ubc.ca/).

Before joining the HCT Lab, I was a [MITACS Globalink Research Intern](https://www.mitacs.ca/en/programs/globalink/globalink-research-internship) in the [Faculty of Medicine and Dentistry](https://www.ualberta.ca/medicine), [Department of Radiology and Diagnostic Imaging](https://www.ualberta.ca/radiology-and-diagnostic-imaging/index.html), [University of Alberta](https://www.ualberta.ca/index.html), Edmonton, advised by [Dr. Lawrence Le](https://sites.google.com/ualberta.ca/lel/home). I obtained my Bachelor of Engineering (B.E) degree in Electrical Engineering from [Jadavpur University](http://www.jaduniv.edu.in/), Kolkata, India, my final year project being under [Prof. Amitava Chatterjee](https://sites.google.com/site/amitavachatterjee1968/). During my junior year, I interned on the 3D Medical Image Processing area, in the [Electronics and Electrical Communication Engineering Department](http://www.iitkgp.ac.in/department/EC) at the [Indian Institute of Technology (IIT Kgp)](http://www.iitkgp.ac.in/), jointly supervised by [Prof. Sudipta Mukhopadhyay](https://sites.google.com/site/smkiitkgp/home) and [Prof. Ashis Kumar Dhara](https://nitdgp.ac.in/faculty/45cb75de-d1d7-4e77-85b6-3110bc4588c6). In my senior year, I worked as a research intern in the Tomography Lab, [Department of Electrical Engineering](http://www.ee.iisc.ac.in/), at the [Indian Institute of Science (IISc Bangalore)](https://www.iisc.ac.in/) under [Prof. Kasi Rajgopal](http://www.ee.iisc.ac.in/people/faculty/kasi/ind.html). 
 
My current research primarily centers around [Deep Learning](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Deep_learning), [Bayesian Inference](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bayesian_inference), [Speech Synthesis](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Speech_synthesis), [Design and Control of Silent Speech Interfaces](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Silent_speech_interface), and [Computer Vision](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Computer_vision). Other interests include [Speech Motor Control](https://www.oxfordscholarship.com/view/10.1093/acprof:oso/9780199235797.001.0001/acprof-9780199235797), [Brain-Computer Interfaces](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Brain%E2%80%93computer_interface), and speech-related [Human-Computer Interfaces](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Human%E2%80%93computer_interaction#Human%E2%80%93computer_interface), [Medical Image Analysis](https://link.springer.com/chapter/10.1007/978-3-540-74658-4_62) and [Reconstruction](https://www.springer.com/gp/book/9783642053689), [Natural Language Processing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Natural_language_processing), etc.
