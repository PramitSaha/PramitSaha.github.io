Hello world! My name is Pramit Saha. I am a final year doctoral student in the Department of Engineering Science at the University of Oxford, fortunate to be supervised by Prof. Alison Noble, CBE FRS FREng FIET. As a part of the Oxford Biomedical Image Analysis (BioMedIA) cluster, my research focuses on Federated Learning, Semi-supervised Learning, and Multimodal Learning towards applications in Computer Vision and Medical Image Analysis. Prior to this, I was a MITACS Globalink Graduate Fellow pursuing masters in the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department, University of British Columbia (UBC), Vancouver, Canada, where I was a Graduate Research Assistant in the Human Communication Technologies Laboratory (HCT Lab), supervised by Prof. Sidney Fels. During my masters, I was awarded the prestigious Young Scientist Award by MICCAI Society for my work on deep learning-based silent speech interfaces.
